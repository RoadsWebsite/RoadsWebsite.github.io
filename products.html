ProRAP for stockpiling

Road Recyclers’ ProRAP technology was developed to meet the needs of agencies that needed an environmentally sound, cost effective recycled asphalt paving material.

Benefits:

Cost: The resulting paving material is cost competitive.

Efficiency/Effectiveness: Longer stockpile life.

Safety: Produced using no environmentally unsafe solvents.

Versatility: Pro-RAP is engineered to meet the needs of the agency end-user, whether as a patch, level-up, or paving material.

Key Details:

Like traditional RAP, ProRAP is made from recycled millings.

Mix designs are done for all jobs to ensure rigorous standard of laboratory and field QC/QA.

Unlike traditional RAP, ProRAP is produced without the use of Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs).

Equipment used for process (usually a pug-mill), allows for easy remote production.

ProRAP for Cold In Place Recycling

Road Recyclers’ Cold In-Place Recycling (CIR) technology uses specially formulated, polymer-modified emulsions and specific mix design methodology to improve upon traditional CIR methods.

Benefits:

Cost: Road Recyclers' process reduces cost through utilizing more of the latent asphalt binder in the existing paving material.

Application: In many cases, Road Recyclers' method improves efficiency by eliminating the need for additional additives, such as lime.

Safety: Road Recyclers' CIR is applied at cooler temperatures than other methods, such as foamed asphalt.

Key Details:

Mobile CIR units can be operated effectively in remote environments.

Held to a rigorous standard of laboratory mix designs and field QC/QA.

In-place material is processed safely without the need for added heat.

CIR typically requires a surface treatment. The appropriate surface treatment may be determined by the mix design.
